Introduction¶

In this project, we will explore the tmdb movies data, containing information about over 10 thousands movies from 1960 to 2015. We will be interested in finding trends associated with the movies that have the more success and how they differ from the movies with less success. In the end, the questions we ll answer here are:

  • What movies genre make the more profit?
  • What movie duration generates the more profit?
  • What release months are more profitable?
  • Does the average grade of a movie predicts its profitability?

Conclusions¶

The genres that get the higher profit rates are Comedy and Drama. In general, average duration movies have more chances than short or long movies to be profitable. July and October seem more favorable to release a movie if you want to make money. Having a rating from 6 to 8 makes the movie more likely to be profitable.

Limitations¶

  • The TMBD Movies dataset used for our analysis may not be ehaustive: our analysis is limited to only the provided data. For example, we are not sure every movie release, from every director is listed here.
  • Exchange rate or currency conversion is not taken into account here, the analysis is limited to the numerical values of revenue
  • Dropping missing or Null values from variables of our interest might skew our analysis and could show unintentional bias towards the relationship being analyzed. etc.
